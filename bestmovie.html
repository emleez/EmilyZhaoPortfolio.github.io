Why This Is My Favorite Movie

I am indifferent to movies most of the time since I might be too picky and busy. For entertainment media, including movies, music, games, et certera, I prefer the ones that grabs my attention and creates its own universe. I watched The Matrix for a school project and out of recommandation and I have to admit that it is the most mesmorizing one that I have ever watched. The amount of effort in the effects and the storyline truely deserves the fame it received. The logic in the movie is innovative and almost flawless, creating the kind of universe you want to be a part of but do not dare to enter.
