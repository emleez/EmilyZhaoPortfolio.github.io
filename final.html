Final Project Using Scratch!

I chose scratch as the language for my final project so it gives me more time to work on it. This project is inspired by Cartoon Network show Steven Universe

I used 5 customized blocks, 7 stages, and 14 sprites. 3 lists are keeping track of the sprite that the player controls: one for the types of gemstones collected, and the other two work together to count the score. The score and the gemstones will both show up on the ending screen. A total of 4 sounds are used for the game as well: one for intro, one for levels 1 to 3, one for levels 4 and 5, and the last one for the ending screen.
